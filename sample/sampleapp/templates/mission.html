Our Story

We're building a transparent, inspiring & personalised way to make change



Why we started?

When you see something that needs to change in your community what do you do?



Shout at the tv that ‘someone should do something about it’? Rant online about how broken the system is and how people just don’t ‘get it’?


What if instead of doing that, we all put up our hand to do something - big or small.


All around the world, there are millions of people who already do just that. We built Chuffed.org for them. To make it incredibly simple for them to fundraise. To make it easy for them to find a community of people who wanted to support them. And to give the rest of us a more transparent and effective way of making the world we want to see.
